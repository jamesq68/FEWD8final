Polices

ORDERING

Orders from approved retailers can be submitted via email. (Approved retailers must provide Model Citizen Press with a business tax ID/EIN.) For email orders please include the contact name, store name, address and phone number. You will receive an email confirmation that we have received your order. Once the order is ready to ship, we will contact you notify you for your credit card information when we are ready to ship. Shipments must be delivered to a verified physical address if at all possible. It is possible to break shipments into partials if required.

MINIMUM ORDER REQUIREMENTS

There is no minimum opening order for wholesale accounts. Each subsequent order minimum is $75. A credit card is required for first orders. We accept PayPal (preferred), Visa, MasterCard, Discover and Amex.

ORDER FULFILLMENT

Wholesale orders are typically fulfilled within 2–3 weeks, although it is not uncommon for them to be finished sooner than this. If for any reason it may take longer, we will contact you with the projected timeline. If you need expedited shipping, please contact us.

SHIPPING

We ship domestic orders via USPS, FedEx Ground or UPS Ground. Shipping fees are paid by buyer and will be added to the invoice. International shipping will be calculated based on the destination.

RETURNS

Email modcitpress@gmail.com before returning any merchandise. Returns are accepted within seven (7) days of receipt of shipment. A restocking fee of 10% will apply. Items must be in new condition with original packaging. Return shipping is the responsibility of the buyer and must have a tracking number. Credits or refunds will be issued after receiving returned items.
